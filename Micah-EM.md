#### Aged Domain Strategy

**Why Aged Domains Are Essential:**
- **Instant Credibility**: 5+ year old domains bypass spam filters 3x better than new domains
- **Faster Deployment**: Skip 2-3 weeks of aggressive warmup required for fresh domains
- **Higher Deliverability**: Aged domains achieve 85-90% inbox rates vs 60-70% for new domains initially
- **Trust Signals**: Email providers treat aged domains as established businesses

**Where to Purchase Aged Domains:**

**Option 1: ExpiredDomains.net**
- Free database with 100,000+ expired domains
- Filter by: Age (5+ years), DA (10+), Clean history
- Cost: $50-500 per domain

**Option 2: GoDaddy Auctions**
- Premium marketplace with verified sellers
- Built-in escrow and transfer protection
- Cost: $100-2,000 per domain

## 4. Sample Value Propositions

### Value Proposition 1: Beauty & Wellness Services
**"Stop Losing $3,000+ Monthly to Processing Fees - Keep 100% of What You Earn"**

*The Problem:* Salons and spas process $60-100K monthly but lose 3-4% to fees while managing tips, memberships, product sales, and appointment deposits.

*Our Solution:* Dual pricing that eliminates your processing fees entirely, integrated booking systems, instant tip payouts, and membership billing automation.

*The Result:* Save $2,400-4,000 monthly ($30-50K annually), streamline operations, and never worry about rate increases again.

### Value Proposition 2: Automotive Services
**"Your Customers Expect to Pay Processing Fees - Stop Absorbing Them"**

*The Problem:* Auto shops handle $50-90K monthly with fleet cards, financing, warranties, and quick approvals while losing thousands to processing fees.

*Our Solution:* Cash discount program (standard in auto industry), all fleet cards accepted, 30-second approvals, and integrated parts ordering.

*The Result:* Add $2,000-3,500 to monthly profit, faster checkout for customers, and complete transaction transparency.

### Value Proposition 3: Specialty Retail (Liquor/Smoke/CBD)
**"Finally, a Processor That Won't Freeze Your Funds or Drop You"**

*The Problem:* High-risk retailers face account closures, 14-day holds, excessive reserves, and 5-10% processing rates from Square/Stripe.

*Our Solution:* Dedicated high-risk accounts with backup MIDs, next-day funding, no reserves after 90 days, and rates starting at 2.9%.

*The Result:* Stable processing without surprises, 40-60% fee reduction, and a partner who understands your business model.
